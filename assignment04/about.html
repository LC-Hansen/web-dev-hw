Taylor Mali is an 11th generation New Yorker and critically acclaimed poet and author. Famous for his witty, and lively "Slam Poety" performances, he is also a novelist and advocate for teachers around the world. His lifes work has been teaching and inspiring, eventually culminating in 2012 with the completion of his 1,000 teachers challenge which was an effort to inspire 1,000 individuals to enter the teaching profession. The poem "What Teachers Make" is his call to action, and by all measures a critical and cultural success.
