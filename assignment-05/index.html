Tasty couscous and shrimp

This is a very quick and tasty meal that can be jazzed up in a number of ways! I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Couscous (measured for serving size)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Shrimp

Now here you can do anything to suit your tastes. I for instance will add a vegetable and sauce.

  • Broccoli
  • Honey
  • Sriracha
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)

    • Directions

    1. Boil water in a medium sauce pan
    2. When water is boiling pour in couscous and 2 tbsp of olive oil
    3. Remove from heat and cover
    4. In another saucepan heat olive oil with garlic
    5. Add shrimp to oil as the garlic starts to brown on edges
    6. Cook shrimp well on one side
    7. Flip shrimp and add chopped broccoli to the pan
    8. Now the couscous should be finished cooking, remove and drizzle with an 8 th cup of honey and Sriracha to taste
    9. Remove shrimp from heat and place on top of the couscous.
    10. Serve in pan with flat bread or on its own!
